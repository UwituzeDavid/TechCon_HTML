Our History

TechCon began in 2010 as a small gathering of developers and tech enthusiasts. Over the years, it has grown into a global event attracting thousands of attendees from around the world. Each year, TechCon evolves to reflect the latest trends and innovations in technology.

Our Mission

TechCon is committed to fostering innovation, collaboration, and education in the tech community. Our mission is to provide a platform where ideas are shared, skills are sharpened, and connections are made to shape the future of technology.

Notable Past Speakers

Dr. Ada Lovelace

Renowned computer scientist and pioneer in algorithmic thinking. Dr. Lovelace spoke on the future of AI and ethics in computing.

Linus Torvalds

Creator of the Linux kernel. Linus shared insights on open-source development and community-driven innovation.

Marissa Mayer

Former CEO of Yahoo and influential tech leader. Marissa discussed leadership in tech and the evolution of user experience design.
